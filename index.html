FAQ

Where do I pay?

Directly on each partner’s website (Viator, eSIM provider, insurer). We’re a curated guide; partners handle payment, tickets, and support.

Can I cancel?

Cancellation rules vary by partner and product. Check the policy on the checkout page before you pay.

Is eSIM data-only?

Most tourist eSIMs are data-only. Use WhatsApp/VoIP for calls. Check plan details for tethering and speed limits.

Do you support Spanish/ARS?

Yes. We can provide a localized version on request.
